Weekend Escapes Near Bangalore

Need a quick break from city life? Here are some of the best short trips from Bangalore — scenic hills, coffee towns, heritage sites, and wildlife spots, all within a few hours’ drive.

Top Weekend Gateways

Nandi Hills

Distance: 60 km | Travel time: 1.5 hours

Perfect for early risers who love scenic sunrises. Ride or drive up winding roads to enjoy misty mornings and panoramic views from Tipu’s Drop and Nandi Temple.

Coorg (Madikeri)

Distance: 270 km | Travel time: 5 to 6 hours

Known as the Scotland of India, Coorg offers coffee plantations, waterfalls, and peaceful homestays. Visit Abbey Falls, Raja’s Seat, and Dubare Elephant Camp.

Chikmagalur

Distance: 250 km | Travel time: 5 hours

A peaceful hill town surrounded by coffee estates and trekking trails. Hike up Mullayanagiri, visit Baba Budangiri, and relax in nature’s calm.

Sakleshpur

Distance: 220 km | Travel time: 4.5 hours

A quiet gem of the Western Ghats with fort ruins, plantations, and beautiful hill views. Explore railway trails and spend a relaxing weekend in cozy stays.

Wayanad (Kerala)

Distance: 280 km | Travel time: 6 hours

Across the border in Kerala, Wayanad offers forested hills, lakes, and waterfalls. Visit Chembra Peak, Edakkal Caves, and Pookode Lake for scenic relaxation.

Hampi

Distance: 340 km | Travel time: 6 to 7 hours

Step back in time at this UNESCO World Heritage Site filled with ancient temples and rock formations. Great for history lovers and photographers alike.

Travel Tips

  • Start early to avoid traffic and enjoy the cool morning hours.
  • Check weather and road conditions before your trip.
  • Book stays in advance during weekends and holidays.
  • Carry essentials like sunscreen, water, and comfortable shoes.
  • Respect local rules and natural surroundings — avoid littering.

Final Thoughts

Bangalore is perfectly located for short breaks in every direction. Whether you’re chasing misty hills, wildlife, or quiet plantation stays, these weekend escapes promise a refreshing change of pace. Plan ahead and make your weekends memorable.
